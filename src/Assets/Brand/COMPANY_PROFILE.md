# Madhouse Wallet — Company Profile

## About

Madhouse Wallet is a modern, security-first digital wallet optimized for fast international payments and remittances. Our focus is on making it simple for users and small businesses to receive and cash out funds from a wide set of global payers.

## Mission

To make cross-border payments effortless and accessible, so individuals and small businesses can receive money quickly and securely.

## Vision

To be the preferred wallet for remittances and merchant payments across emerging markets, offering local cashout options and a frictionless user experience.

## Product Summary

- Effortless payments: "Effortlessly receive international payments" is the primary on-site headline — the product is optimized for inbound payments from many countries.
- Cashout options: Cash out to bank accounts or mobile money.
- Security-first: Passkey-style and device-backed auth patterns and security engineering baked into the product flows.
- Integrations: Local payment rails and partner integrations for on/off ramps and settlement.

## Key Features (from the landing page)

- Secure by Design — Seamless customer experience with enhanced security features like passkey authentication.
- Accept Payments — Expand customer base by accepting payments from different countries.
- Easily Invest — Invest in bitcoin or earn interest on savings.
- Cash out Anytime — Transfer funds to bank accounts or mobile money.

## Target Audience

- Individuals receiving remittances across borders.
- Small businesses and merchants who need low-cost, reliable payment rails.
- Users new to crypto looking for a friendly, secure entry point.
