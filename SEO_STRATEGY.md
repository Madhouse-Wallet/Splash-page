# Advanced SEO Strategy - Madhouse Wallet
## Industry-Leading SEO Implementation Plan

### Target Markets
- **Primary:** Kenya, Nigeria, India
- **Secondary:** Global emerging markets

### Target Audience
- Small business owners receiving international payments
- Freelancers and entrepreneurs in emerging markets
- Merchants accepting cross-border payments
- Individuals receiving remittances from abroad

1. **Payment App Keywords:**
   - payment app for small business
   - business payment app Kenya/Nigeria/India
   - accept international payments
   - merchant payment app
   - digital payment solution for small business

2. **Remittance Keywords:**
   - remittance app Kenya/Nigeria/India
   - send money to Kenya/Nigeria/India
   - receive money from abroad
   - international money transfer app
   - cross-border remittance

3. **Long-Tail Keywords:**
   - how to receive international payments in Kenya

4. **Competitor Keywords:**

   - remittance app like Remitly
   - payment solution for African businesses

### Advanced SEO Tactics Implemented

#### 1. Geo-Targeting
- ✅ Hreflang tags for Kenya, Nigeria, India
- ✅ Geo-specific meta tags
- ✅ LocalBusiness schema for each country
- ✅ Country-specific keywords in content

#### 2. Structured Data (Schema.org)
- ✅ Organization schema
- ✅ SoftwareApplication schema
- ✅ FinancialService schema
- ✅ FAQ schema (common questions)
- ✅ HowTo schema (setup guide)
- ✅ LocalBusiness schema (per country)
- ✅ AggregateRating schema
- ✅ Offer schema with regional benefits

#### 3. Content Optimization
- ✅ H1 with primary keyword
- ✅ H2/H3 with semantic variations
- ✅ Alt text with geo-keywords
- ✅ Meta descriptions with CTAs
- ✅ Long-tail keyword integration

#### 4. Technical SEO
- ✅ Mobile-first optimization
- ✅ Page speed optimization indicators
- ✅ Structured navigation
- ✅ Canonical tags
- ✅ Robots.txt with AI crawler access

#### 5. Trust Signals
- ✅ Security badges
- ✅ Customer ratings
- ✅ Supported countries listing
- ✅ Contact information
