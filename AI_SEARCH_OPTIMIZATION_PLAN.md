## üéØ Understanding AI Search vs Traditional SEO

### Traditional SEO Focuses On:
- Keywords and keyword density
- Backlinks and domain authority
- Page speed and technical performance
- Meta tags and titles

### AI Search Focuses On:
- **Natural Language Understanding** - Conversational queries
- **Structured Data** - Machine-readable information
- **Entity Recognition** - Clear identification of what/who/where
- **Contextual Relationships** - How entities relate to each other
- **Question-Answer Matching** - Direct answers to user questions
- **Semantic Understanding** - Meaning over keywords
- **Factual Accuracy** - Verifiable, clear information
